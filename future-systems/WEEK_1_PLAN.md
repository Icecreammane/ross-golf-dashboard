## Common Challenges & Solutions

### "I forgot to log actions"
**Solution:** Set hourly reminder for first week. After 7 days, it becomes automatic.

### "Voice commands feel awkward"
**Solution:** Stick with it for 5 days. Awkward ‚Üí Natural around day 4-5.

### "Not getting enough replies to learn"
**Solution:** Focus on sending volume. Need 50+ sends. Reply rate will vary but patterns emerge with volume.

### "Too much data to track"
**Solution:** Systems do the tracking. You just log actions. Analysis is automated.
