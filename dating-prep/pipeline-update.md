## Pipeline Stages (Reference)

Your dating pipeline should look something like this:

1. **Prospects** - Women you're interested in / talking to online
2. **Active Chat** - Ongoing conversation, building rapport
3. **Date Planned** - Date scheduled (like Monday's date)
4. **First Date** - Post first date, evaluating fit
5. **Follow-up** - Want to see again, coordinating second date
6. **Dating** - Seeing regularly (2+ dates)
7. **Exclusive** - Committed relationship
8. **Done** - Not a match / ended

## Data to Track (For Learning)

**For every date, log:**

- Date/time
- Location/activity
- Duration
- Energy level (1-10)
- Attraction (1-10)
- Conversation quality (1-10)
- Green flags (list)
- Red flags (list)
- Outcome (second date? not interested?)

**Why track this?**

Over time, you'll see patterns:

- "I do better on activity dates than dinner dates"
- "Women I meet through [source] are better fits"
- "I'm attracted to [type], but they're always [red flag]"
- "Dates that go [X] hours tend to lead to second dates"

Data = learning = better decisions.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### 1. Chasing Low-Interest Women
**Symptom:** She's not texting back, gave short answers, seemed distracted.  
**Fix:** Move on. Don't chase. Pursue women who are enthusiastic about you.

### 2. Ignoring Red Flags
**Symptom:** "She was rude to the server, but maybe she was just having a bad day..."  
**Fix:** Red flags on date 1 = red flags forever. Trust your gut.

### 3. Settling Because "It's Hard to Meet People"
**Symptom:** "She's not really what I'm looking for, but dating is hard, so maybe..."  
**Fix:** Being alone is better than being with the wrong person. Don't settle.

### 4. Not Learning from Bad Dates
**Symptom:** Same bad date over and over with different women.  
**Fix:** Track patterns. If you keep attracting the same red flags, you're screening wrong.

### 5. Moving Too Fast
**Symptom:** Great first date â†’ immediately planning date 3, 4, 5.  
**Fix:** Slow down. Give it 3-4 dates before deciding if this is serious.

## Success Metrics

**What does "success" look like in dating?**

It's not "find a girlfriend ASAP."

It's:

1. **Consistent dating practice** (1-2 dates per week)
2. **Getting better at conversation** (less awkward over time)
3. **Clear standards** (knowing what you want, filtering faster)
4. **No-drama exits** (ending things cleanly when not a fit)
5. **Eventually meeting someone great** (because you've practiced and clarified)

**Monday's date is one rep.** Whether it goes great or terrible, it's practice. Keep going.
