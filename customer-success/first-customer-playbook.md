## CHURN PREVENTION

### Early Warning Signs (Monitor These)
1. **Hasn't logged in 3 days** (was logging daily)
2. **Hasn't logged food in 5 days** (was logging regularly)
3. **Only using free trial, 1 day left, no payment method added**

### Intervention Emails

## SUCCESS METRICS (Track These)

### Conversion Metrics
- **Trial ‚Üí Paid:** Target 30% (industry average: 15-25%)
- **Month 1 retention:** Target 80%
- **Month 3 retention:** Target 60%
- **Churn rate:** Target <10% monthly

### Engagement Metrics
- **Daily active users:** Target 60% of paid users log daily
- **Weekly active users:** Target 90% of paid users log weekly
- **Average session duration:** Target 2-3 minutes (fast logging = good)

### Advocacy Metrics
- **Testimonials collected:** Target 5 in first month
- **Referrals per customer:** Target 0.5 (1 referral per 2 customers)
- **NPS (Net Promoter Score):** Target 50+ (9-10 rating)
