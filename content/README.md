## Key Insights

### Faceless Content (9 ideas)
- Protects your privacy
- Easier to film (no makeup, lighting concerns)
- Focus on value, not personality
- Perfect for fitness/gym content

### 2016 vs 2026 Format (Trending Now)
- Nostalgic content is viral gold in 2026
- Easy to make: split screen, before/after
- Relatable to everyone 25-35 years old

### Authenticity Over Production
- Your real journey > fake guru content
- Raw iPhone footage often outperforms polished videos
- People can smell BS from a mile away

### TikTok (Primary)
- Best for trends and faceless content

- Post 2x daily for maximum growth

### Instagram Reels (Secondary)
- Same content as TikTok

### YouTube Shorts (Bonus)

- Easier to monetize long-term

- Build credibility, drive traffic
