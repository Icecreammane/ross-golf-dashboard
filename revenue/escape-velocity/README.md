# Escape Velocity Calculator

**Your personalized financial freedom dashboard.**

## What It Does

Tracks your journey to financial independence with three key metrics:
- **Days Until $500 MRR** - When your side income hits the "quit-ready" threshold
- **Florida Fund Progress** - Countdown to your $50k beach volleyball fund
- **Financial Freedom Date** - When side income can cover 80% of your expenses

## How to Use

1. **Open in browser:** `calculator.html`
2. **Enter your numbers:**
   - Current salary (annual)
   - Current side income (monthly)
   - Savings rate (%)
   - Monthly expenses
   - Current savings

3. **Watch the magic:**
   - Real-time calculations
   - Visual progress bars
   - Milestone tracking

4. **Play with scenarios:**
   - Click "What-If" buttons to see how adding income streams accelerates your timeline
   - Try different combinations

## Features

- **Auto-saves** your data to browser (won't lose progress)
- **Mobile-optimized** - check on your phone anytime
- **Visual progress bars** - see how close you are
- **Milestone badges** - celebrate wins along the way

## What-If Scenarios

Pre-loaded scenarios based on your Deal Flow opportunities:
- Launch Affiliate Program: +$300/mo
- Pet Food Consulting: +$500/mo
- Viral Content Success: +$800/mo
- Arbitrage Hustle: +$400/mo
- Beach Volleyball Coaching: +$600/mo
- Increase Savings Rate: +10%

## Update Your Numbers

As you make progress:
1. Update "Current Side Income" when deals close
2. Update "Current Savings" monthly
3. Watch your freedom date move closer

## The Math

- **$500 MRR:** Industry threshold for "quit-ready" side business
- **$50k Florida Fund:** Down payment + moving costs for Florida beach life
- **Freedom Date:** When side income = 80% of expenses (conservative 25x multiplier)
