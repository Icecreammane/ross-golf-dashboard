## Common Questions

**Q: Do I need to film myself?**
A: No! 9 of the 30 content ideas are "faceless" - just gym footage, equipment shots, etc. Perfect for privacy.

**Q: What if I don't know how to edit videos?**
A: Download CapCut (free). It's stupid easy. Literally just trim clips and add text. That's 90% of viral content.

**Q: Which opportunity should I start with?**
A: Pick one that:
1. You can start TODAY (not in 6 months)
2. Costs less than $500 to start
3. Excites you even a little bit

**Q: How do I update the Escape Velocity Calculator?**
A: Just change the numbers in the input fields. It auto-saves. Update it weekly as you make progress.

**Q: Can I add my own opportunities to Deal Flow?**
A: Yes! Edit `opportunities.json` - just follow the format of existing entries.
