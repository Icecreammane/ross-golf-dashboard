## Revenue Model Comparison

### One-Time Sale (Gumroad):
- **Pro:** Simple, passive income
- **Con:** No recurring revenue

**Example:**  
100 sales @ $24 = $2,400 (Year 1)  
Then: 20 sales/month @ $24 = $480/month passive

### Subscription (Patreon):
- **Pro:** Recurring revenue
- **Con:** Must create new templates monthly

**Example:**  
50 subscribers @ $9/month = $450/month  
Annual: $5,400

### Hybrid Model:
- Core templates: $19-29 one-time
- "Template Club": $9/month for new templates
- **Best of both worlds**
