# AI Concierge Service - Market Research

**Research Date:** Feb 5, 2026
**Analyst:** Builder Agent

---

## Executive Summary

The AI concierge/personal assistant market is experiencing explosive growth in 2026, with established competitors ranging from $20/month SaaS tools to $50k+ enterprise implementations. The market shows clear gaps for premium personal concierge services targeting affluent individuals and executives.

**Key Findings:**
- Market is fragmented between low-cost SaaS ($20-100/mo) and enterprise solutions ($50k+)
- **Sweet spot opportunity:** Premium personal concierge service at $5-10k setup
- 78% of agencies struggle with AI service pricing (opportunity for clarity)
- Value-based pricing commands 2.3x higher profitability vs project-based
- Recurring revenue models provide predictability and higher valuations

#### 1. **Concierge AI** (SaaS Platform)

- **Target:** SMBs, self-service
- **Positioning:** Low-cost chatbot/automation tool

#### 2. **1mind Virtual Assistants** (Service-Based)
- **Pricing:** Undisclosed, enterprise focus
- **Target:** Business operations (sales, marketing, customer success)
- **Positioning:** Customizable virtual assistants
- **Weakness:** B2B focused, not personal concierge

#### 3. **A2Z Virtual Assistant Services** (Human-Powered)
- **Pricing:** Unknown, appears mid-market
- **Target:** Small businesses
- **Positioning:** "Honest, dedicated, hard working" humans
- **Weakness:** Not AI-powered, limited by human hours

#### 4. **Zendesk Customer Service AI**
- **Pricing:** Enterprise licensing
- **Target:** Large organizations
- **Positioning:** Customer service automation
- **Weakness:** Not personal assistant focused

## Pricing Intelligence

### Industry Standard Models (2026)

**1. Value-Based Pricing** (Recommended)
- Price at **15-30% of annual value created**
- Example: If AI saves client $50k/year ‚Üí charge $10-15k setup
- Requires ROI calculation and proof

**2. Subscription/Retainer Models**
- Basic Maintenance: $2,000-$5,000/month
- Active Management: $5,000-$15,000/month
- Full-Service Partnership: $15,000+/month

**3. Hybrid Models** (Most Popular)
- Setup fee + ongoing revenue share
- Tiered packages with upsells
- Platform license + services

**4. Performance-Based**
- 10-30% revenue share
- 20-40% of documented cost savings

### Geographic Variations
- Major US markets: 20-40% premium
- Remote service delivery allows premium pricing from anywhere
- Ross's location (likely non-major market) = opportunity for arbitrage
