## RISK FACTORS

### High Risk (Could Kill Growth)
- **Churn rate >15%:** Product isn't sticky, users leave quickly
- **Conversion rate <15%:** Trials don't convert, leaky funnel
- **Signup growth plateaus:** No word-of-mouth, marketing exhausted
- **Competitor launches similar:** MFP releases "simple mode"

### Medium Risk (Could Slow Growth)
- **Technical issues:** Downtime, bugs, bad user experience
- **Negative reviews:** Bad Reddit post, influencer criticism
- **Pricing resistance:** Users won't pay $10/month
- **Feature requests overwhelm:** Can't build fast enough

### Low Risk (Manageable)
- **Server costs increase:** Upgrade hosting as users grow
- **Support burden:** More users = more support emails
- **Stripe fees:** Predictable, just cost of business

## MITIGATION STRATEGIES

### To Reduce Churn:
- **Onboarding excellence:** Get users to first success fast
- **Regular engagement:** Email tips, celebrate milestones
- **Feature iteration:** Build what users actually request
- **Community:** Build sense of belonging (FitTrack users club)

### To Improve Conversion:
- **Social proof:** Testimonials, case studies, user counts
- **Reduce friction:** Simplify signup, faster onboarding
- **Trial optimization:** Ensure users experience value in 7 days
- **Clear CTA:** Make it easy to subscribe

### To Sustain Growth:
- **Content marketing:** SEO blog posts compound over time
- **Referral program:** Incentivize word-of-mouth
- **Partnerships:** Gyms, coaches, influencers
- **Product excellence:** Best product = best marketing
