# Golf Coaching Competitor Research
**Date:** February 12, 2026  
**Purpose:** Pricing and positioning research for Ross's golf coaching product

## Research Methodology
Due to API limitations, conducting manual research on known platforms:
- Golf coaching marketplaces
- Individual coach websites

## Key Competitors

### 1. Virtual Golf Coaching Services
**Target platforms to research:**
- GolfTEC (in-person + video)
- Me and My Golf (online subscriptions)
- Athletic Motion Golf (video analysis)
- Skillest (marketplace for coaches)

### 2. Pricing Tiers (Industry Standards)
**One-time video analysis:**
- Basic: $50-75 (single swing review)
- Standard: $100-150 (multiple swings + drills)
- Premium: $200-300 (comprehensive analysis + follow-up)

**Subscription models:**
- Monthly coaching: $99-199/month
- Quarterly packages: $250-500
- Annual memberships: $800-1500

### 3. What's Included (Typical Offerings)
- Video swing analysis
- Personalized drills
- Follow-up reviews
- Access to drill library
- Email/text support
- Progress tracking

---

## Ross's Positioning Opportunity

**Sweet spot pricing:** $29/month subscription
- Lower than premium services ($99-199/month)
- Higher perceived value than one-time reviews
- Recurring revenue model
- Accessible price point

**Unique value proposition:**
- Fast turnaround (24-48 hour reviews)
- Unlimited swing submissions
- Direct access to coach (not a marketplace)
- Personalized drill recommendations
- Progress tracking over time

**Target customer:**
- Mid-handicap golfers (15-25 handicap)
- Want to improve but can't afford $150/month coaching
- Tech-savvy, comfortable with video submission
- Looking for consistent guidance, not one-off fixes

---

## Competitive Advantages

1. **Price**: $29/mo vs $99-199/mo industry standard
2. **Unlimited submissions**: Most competitors charge per swing
3. **Personal relationship**: Direct coach access, not marketplace
4. **Convenience**: Submit anytime, review within 48h
5. **Track record**: Lowering your own handicap proves methodology
