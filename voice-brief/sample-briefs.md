## üéØ DESIGN NOTES

### Voice Characteristics
- **Tone:** Conversational, motivational, direct
- **Pacing:** Natural pauses between sections (2-3 seconds)
- **Emphasis:** Key numbers, action items, wins
- **Length:** 1:45 - 2:45 minutes (optimal attention span)

### Content Modules (Dynamic)

#### Always Include:
1. **Greeting** - Time, date, context-aware message
2. **Day overview** - Weather, calendar, obligations
3. **Key metrics** - Florida Fund, revenue, fitness
4. **Action items** - Today's priorities
5. **Closing** - Motivational wrap-up

#### Context-Dependent:
- **Weekday:** System health, meetings, tight schedule
- **Weekend:** Relaxed tone, build suggestions, project ideas
- **Build day:** Night shift recap, strategic insights
- **Monday:** Week kickoff, goal setting
- **Friday:** Week review, weekend preview

#### Personalization Triggers:
- **After milestone:** Celebrate wins, acknowledge progress
- **After setback:** Encouraging tone, remind of resilience
- **Streak at risk:** Gentle reminder, motivational push
- **New opportunity:** Market trend, side hustle idea
- **Goal approaching:** "You're 87% of the way there"

## üîä AUDIO PRODUCTION NOTES

### Voice Settings (ElevenLabs or similar)
- **Voice:** Professional male, mid-30s tone
- **Stability:** 0.6 (natural variation)
- **Clarity:** 0.8 (clear, understandable)
- **Style:** 0.4 (conversational, not robotic)

### Pauses & Pacing
- Short pause (1 sec): Between sentences within a section
- Medium pause (2-3 sec): Between sections
- Long pause (4 sec): Before key action items or closing

### Emphasis
- **Numbers:** Slightly louder, slower ($18,420, 47-day streak)
- **Action verbs:** Confident tone (ship, launch, complete)
- **Names:** Personal touch (Ross, Sarah)

## üìä METRICS TO TRACK

### Personalization Accuracy
- **Relevant insights:** Are market trends actually useful?
