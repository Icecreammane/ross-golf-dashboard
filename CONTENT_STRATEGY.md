## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Headline is 80% of success**
   - Use numbers ("5 Ways," "3 Secrets")
   - Create curiosity gap
   - Promise specific value

2. **Hook in 30 seconds**
   - First paragraph must grab attention
   - Start with bold claim or story

3. **Optimize for skimmers**
   - Use subheaders liberally
   - Bold key points
   - Short paragraphs (2-3 lines)

4. **Add credibility**
   - Your Mars experience = authority
   - Include data/statistics
   - Link to sources

5. **End with CTA**
   - "If you found this valuable, give it a clap"
   - "Follow for more inside industry knowledge"

6. **Timing matters**
   - Publish Tuesday-Thursday mornings
   - Seasonal content (fantasy football in August)

7. **Engage with comments**
   - Reply to every comment first 24 hours
   - Builds algorithm favor
